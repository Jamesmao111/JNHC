Reflection

Initially, at the start of this project I aspired for a theme correlating to voices, but I want something unique and special that non of my peers were investigating.

This eventually took me to the theme the manipulation of voices to form artificial consensus which was eventually narrowed down into a singular figure, Edward Bernays.

Throughout my research I developed a deeper understanding of how the public can be coerced into a perspective with invaluable insights from primary sources such as Propaganda and Crystallising Public opinion, written by Edward Bernay's himself.

The few pages I could read were instrumental in my understanding of the powerful figures that exist behind advertisements and the impacts of these engineered elements of my daily life and desires.
